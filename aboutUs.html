About Us

Thinking Cube is a library website created by readers, for readers.

We have proudly provided access to a variety of genres, authors, and topics, the ability to search for specific books and the ability to purchase books directly from the library since the website was first created in 2012. The library is constantly growing, with new books being added every day and the number of users served becoming well over 50 thousand!
Thinking Cube has and will continue to be a great way for readers to discover new books and genres they love, and for authors to get the recognition they deserve.
